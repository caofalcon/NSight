Our Mission Statement

To provide academic, social and personal support for first- and second-year students in Engineering Science in order to enrich their experience at the University of Toronto, and in particular within the Engineering Science community.


NSight Network

We connect first and second year Engineering Science students with important resources, programs, and information available on and off campus.

NSight Mentorship

Every mentee will be paired with an upper-year Engineering Science student. You can go to your NSight Mentor with any concerns and questions regarding your transition to Engineering Science and university life.

NSight Organized Events

We encourage you to get involved in our organized events to develop friendships, network with upper years and benefit from opportunities uniquely available to Engineering Science students.
